About BIGJOHN

The story behind the music

BIGJOHN is a dynamic singer-songwriter whose music blends contemporary pop with soulful melodies and heartfelt lyrics. With over a decade of experience in the music industry, BIGJOHN has captivated audiences worldwide with powerful performances and authentic storytelling.

From intimate acoustic sessions to high-energy concerts, Alex's versatility as an artist shines through every performance. The journey began in small coffee shops and has evolved into sold-out venues across the country.
